## 🔄 Key Changes Documented

### Your Assistant Tab
- **Before**: Could upload documents, stored chats in sidebar
- **After**: Pure stateless AI chat, no documents, no sidebar history
- **Benefit**: Simpler, faster, no confusion

### Knowledge Hub Tab
- **Before**: One question, one answer, clear to refresh
- **After**: Infinite multi-turn Q&A, full conversation history persists
- **Benefit**: Better for deep document exploration

### Meeting Assistant Tab
- **Before**: Follow-up section always visible
- **After**: Follow-up section only appears after summary generated
- **Benefit**: Better workflow, less confusing UI

### All Clear/Clean Buttons
- **Before**: Would throw StreamlitAPIException
- **After**: All work perfectly without errors
- **Benefit**: Stable, reliable app
