Title: Service Level Agreement (SLA) Policy

Overview:
This document defines the expected response and resolution times for IT support services.

Response Time Targets:
- P1 (Critical): 15 minutes
- P2 (High): 1 hour
- P3 (Medium): 4 hours
- P4 (Low): 8 hours

Resolution Time Targets:
- P1 (Critical): 4 hours
- P2 (High): 8 hours
- P3 (Medium): 24 hours
- P4 (Low): 48 hours

Definitions:
- Response Time: Time taken to acknowledge the issue.
- Resolution Time: Time taken to resolve the issue.

SLA Breach:
If resolution time exceeds the defined SLA, it is considered a breach.

Escalation Rules:
- P1 incidents must be immediately escalated to senior support.
- Repeated SLA breaches must be reviewed by service management.

Service Hours:
- Standard support: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM
- Critical support: 24/7

Reporting:
Monthly SLA performance reports should be generated and reviewed.

Continuous Improvement:
SLA metrics should be used to identify improvement opportunities in service delivery.
