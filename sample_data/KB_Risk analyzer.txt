Meeting: Ticketing System Migration – Initial Discussion

Participants: IT Manager, Service Manager, Dev Team, Business Stakeholders

Summary:
The company plans to migrate the current ticketing system (manual + basic Azure DevOps setup) to a more advanced platform with SLA tracking, automated ticket routing, and email integration.

Key points discussed:
- Current system lacks SLA visibility and reporting capabilities
- Manual ticket assignment is causing delays and inefficiencies
- Business requires faster response times and better tracking of incidents and service requests
- Integration with Outlook email system is required
- Historical ticket data migration is required
- No clear ownership defined yet for SLA monitoring
- Users are not fully trained on current ticketing processes
- Budget constraints may limit customization
- Timeline proposed: 3 months for full migration

Open concerns:
- Data migration complexity and data quality
- Risk of service disruption during migration
- Lack of clear process documentation
- Dependency on external vendor for new platform implementation
