Title: Password Reset Procedure

Overview:
This document explains how users can reset their password in the corporate system.

Steps for Password Reset:
1. Go to the login page of the system.
2. Click on "Forgot Password".
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Check your email inbox for the reset link.
5. Click the link and create a new password.
6. Ensure the password meets the following criteria:
   - Minimum 8 characters
   - At least one uppercase letter
   - At least one number
   - At least one special character

Common Issues:
- If the email is not received, check the spam folder.
- If the link is expired, request a new password reset.
- If the account is locked after multiple failed attempts, contact IT support.

Security Notes:
- Never share your password with anyone.
- Change your password every 90 days.

Escalation:
If the issue persists, raise a ticket under "Access Management" category.
